I am currently a web developer at Linked by Air, a design and technology studio based in Brooklyn, New York. My formal background is in graphic design, and I hold a B.F.A. in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design. I devoted the majority of my work there to designing and programming in web-based contexts. Upon leaving university I started work as a designer, but switched to development shortly thereafter.
